This role calls for a person with basic project management skills and knowledge to perform complex tasks - independently as well as in coordination with other team members - on programs / projects involving one or more work sites and applications. It is associated with the programmatic execution of the EMS programs, an integral part of the Aviation business Facilitating day-to-day team programmatic and reporting Analyzes and validates costs and supports in the status of a program/ project using basic understanding of cost buildup and budgeting.
